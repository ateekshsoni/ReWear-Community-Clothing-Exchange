Common Questions

How do I earn points?

You earn points by listing items, completing swaps, and referring friends to the platform. Each successful swap earns you 50-100 points depending on the item value.

What if an item doesn't fit?

We encourage detailed size information and offer a 7-day return policy for point redemptions. For direct swaps, we recommend discussing sizing details with the other member beforehand.

How do I arrange item exchanges?

Our messaging system connects you directly with other users to coordinate safe meetups or shipping. We provide guidelines for secure exchanges and recommend meeting in public places.

What items are not allowed?

We don't allow undergarments, heavily damaged items, or items that violate our community guidelines. All items must be clean and in good condition.
